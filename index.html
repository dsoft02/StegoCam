StegoCam Features

Explore StegoCam features below.

Message Hiding

With the Message Hiding feature in StegoCam, you can securely hide messages within images, ensuring sensitive information remains confidential and protected. Whether it's a text message, a secret note, or any other form of text-based communication, StegoCam provides a seamless and secure way to embed the message within an image of your choice.

Stego History

StegoCam keeps track of all your steganographic activities with its Stego History feature. Easily access and manage your previous message hiding operations for quick reference or extraction. The Stego History feature provides a convenient way to review your past activities and retrieve any hidden messages

Secure PIN and Fingerprint Access

StegoCam offers enhanced security for accessing the app through its Secure PIN and Fingerprint Biometric feature. With this feature, you can protect your hidden messages with a personalized PIN code and utilize the convenience and added security of fingerprint biometrics for authentication.

Project Inspiration

StegoCam is not just a project born out of academic requirements, but rather a passion project inspired by personal experiences and a desire to explore the exciting field of data security. The idea originated from a fascination with the concept of steganography and the hidden communication it enables. The project was envisioned as a means to empower individuals with a tool to protect their private messages, providing them with a sense of security and control in an increasingly digital world.

Project Timeline

The journey of StegoCam started during the tenure at Holberton School, where the project was developed as part of the Portfolio Project. The development process spanned through three(3) weeks, involving meticulous planning, research, and implementation of various features. The project underwent multiple iterations to refine its functionality, user experience, and security aspects.
